HealthyEating — UX Plan

Inspiration-driven nutrition app with AI coaching

Design Principles
Apple Health-inspired clarity with a warm, encouraging coaching tone.
🫧

Breathable

Generous white space, no clutter. Every pixel earns its place. Information density without visual noise.

🎯

Glanceable

Key data (macros, today's meals, progress) visible in under 2 seconds. Rings and bars over raw numbers.

🌱

Encouraging

AI coach uses warm, positive framing. "You're 20g away from your protein goal" not "You failed your target."

✨

Delightful

Subtle micro-animations on progress rings, smooth transitions, haptic feedback on key actions.

Key User Flows
How the AI coach weaves through the core experience.
🔄 Weekly Flow
Sunday Evening

Plan the Week

Browse Discover for inspiration → drag recipes into Plan → AI fills gaps to hit macro targets → tap "Generate Shopping List"

Sunday / Monday

Shop & Prep

Check off items in Shop tab → start a Prep Session → batch cook meals → mark as prepped with storage notes

Each Day

Today Dashboard

See planned meals → track macros as you eat → get coach nudges ("add a handful of walnuts for omega-3")

After Cooking

Log & Learn

Rate meals → give feedback on AI suggestions → cooking log builds your taste profile → AI gets smarter over time

🧠 AI Coach Touchpoints
Today Tab

Morning Nudge

Personalised tip based on yesterday's nutrition gaps. Positive framing, one actionable suggestion.

Discover Tab

Smart Recommendations

Recipes ranked by macro gap analysis, taste profile, cooking history. "Because you liked X" explanations.

Plan Tab

Fill the Gaps

"AI Fill Gaps" button suggests meals for empty slots that balance the day's macros against your targets.

Recipe Detail

Smart Swaps

"Swap chicken for tofu to make this vegan" — contextual ingredient substitutions with updated macros.
